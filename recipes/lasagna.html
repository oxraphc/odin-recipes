Lasagna Recipe
Description
This lasagna recipe is one of the most rated lasagna recipe on allrecipes.com, you may follow this recipe and cook a tasty lasagna.
Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
Steps
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna with the order:
    • Meat sauce
    • Noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Mozzarella slices
    • Meat sauce
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
